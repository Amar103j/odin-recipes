Pizza Recipe

This homemade pizza recipe is perfect for pizza night with your family or friends. The crust is crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, making it the ideal base for your favorite toppings.

Whether you prefer classic pepperoni or a mix of colorful vegetables, this recipe is flexible and fun to customize. Serve it fresh from the oven and enjoy every slice!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water (110°F)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)
  1. In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Knead until a soft dough forms.
  3. Cover and let the dough rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  6. Place the dough on a greased baking tray or pizza stone.
  7. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the crust.
  8. Add mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  9. Bake for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  10. Let cool slightly, then slice and serve!
