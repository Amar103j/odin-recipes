Chicken Soup Recipe

This hearty chicken soup is the perfect comfort food for chilly days or when you're feeling under the weather. Packed with tender chicken, fresh vegetables, and aromatic herbs, it's a simple yet nourishing dish the whole family will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups cooked shredded chicken
  • 1 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup egg noodles or rice (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and garlic. Cook until softened, about 3 minutes.
  3. Add carrots and celery. Cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  5. Add the cooked shredded chicken, salt, pepper, thyme, and bay leaf.
  6. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes to blend flavors.
  7. If using noodles or rice, add them now and cook until tender.
  8. Remove the bay leaf. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  9. Ladle soup into bowls, garnish with parsley, and serve hot.
  10. Enjoy your warm and comforting chicken soup!
