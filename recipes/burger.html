Burger Recipe

Description

This juicy homemade burger is perfect for a quick lunch or a weekend cookout. Made with simple ingredients, it's loaded with flavor and can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Whether you're grilling outside or cooking in a pan, this burger comes together in no time and is sure to satisfy any appetite.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter (for cooking)
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion (optional)
  • Cheese slice (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, or other sauces

Steps

  1. In a bowl, gently mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Form into a patty.
  2. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat and add oil or butter.
  3. Cook the burger patty for 3â€“4 minutes per side, or until fully cooked to your liking.
  4. If using cheese, place it on the patty during the last minute of cooking and cover to melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger bun lightly in the pan or toaster.
  6. Assemble the burger: place the patty on the bottom bun, add lettuce, tomato, onion, and sauces as desired, then top with the other bun half.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
